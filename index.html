Write-up

I used two datasets in this homework. The first one is Annual Estimates of the Resident Population for the United States, Regions, States, and Puerto Rico.

It records the estimated population of each state in United States for 10 years, so we can draw plots easily. I drew two plots to show the population growth in Illinois state over the last ten years.

Second one is cars dataset in JSON format.

This dataset records the information of cars produced by different companies, including name, cylinders, horsepower, weight, origin country etc. I drew a scatter plot to show the relationship between horsepower and the miles per gallon. We can easily find that, cars with higher horsepower tend to consume more fuel. Line chart is not suitable here, because many cars have the same horsepower.

The second plot is a bar chart with aggregation showing the total numbers of cars produced by each country. By counting the number of occurrences of a country, we can find the United States has produced the most cars.
